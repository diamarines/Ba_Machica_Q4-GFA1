TATAG IP Students Foundation, Inc. is the main implementing organization of the TATAG IP Students Support Program. Our main beneficiaries are the children of former rebels from the Geographically Isolated and Deprived Areas of Talaingod, Davao del Norte. The TATAG IP Students Support Program works to establish favorable conditions conducive for learning and education, skills development, moral and values formation and physical and mental development of the IP Students. For a developed progressive and conflict-resilient IP communities in Talaingod, Davao del Norte. I am supporting this foundation because of its vision to have a transformed, educated, and learned Ata Manobo tribe who are peace bearers, great leaders and productive members of the society. And its mission to provide sustainable support necessary for the quality education and proper learning of the indigent Ata manobo youth students in Talaingod, Davao del Norte.
